Motion Gaming

Your Gateway to Interactive Gaming Experiences

Motion Gaming is a leading technology company in Israel specializing in creating video games controlled by hand movements using advanced camera motion detection technology. The company was founded by Shay Goldenberg. Its goal is to change how people engage with video games by providing a unique and immersive gaming experience. Through hand motion detection, our games allow players to control characters, objects, and environments in the virtual world using natural hand movements, creating an unprecedented level of engagement and interaction. The emphasis is on delivering conceptual, innovative, and fascinating gaming experiences to our players.
